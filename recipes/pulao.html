Pulao

Description :

Called by various names such as pilaf or pulav, Vegetable Pulao is one of the most common rice dishes in India, the other being Veg Biryani.

Ingredients :

  • Basmati Rice
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Vegetable(potatos,onion,tomato etc)
  • garlics

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients and equipment.
  2. Heat cooking oil or butter in a pan.
  3. Cook onions, garlic, or other aromatics.
  4. Brown meat, poultry, or seafood if using.
  5. Add veggies, spices, and other ingredients.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and spices per recipe.
  7. Pour in broth, water, or sauce as needed.
  8. Cook until ingredients are done.
  9. Adjust seasoning as necessary.
  10. Plate the dish and garnish if desired.
