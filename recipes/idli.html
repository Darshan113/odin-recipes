Idli

Description:

Idli is a popular South Indian steamed rice cake, typically served with coconut chutney and sambar. It's a healthy and light breakfast option.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup idli rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing

Steps:

  1. Wash and soak idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked urad dal into a smooth batter, and the rice into a slightly coarse batter.
  3. Mix the two batters, add salt, and let it ferment for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  4. Grease idli molds, pour the batter, and steam for 10-12 minutes until idlis are cooked.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
