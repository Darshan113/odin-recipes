Paneer Tikka

Description:

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer cubes,
bell peppers, and onions, grilled or baked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon tikka masala or tandoori masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Bell peppers and onions (sliced into cubes)
  • Skewers

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, tikka masala, red chili powder, turmeric, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes, bell peppers, and onions to the marinade. Coat them well and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Thread marinated paneer, peppers, and onions onto skewers.
  4. Grill or bake in the oven until paneer is charred and veggies are tender.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
